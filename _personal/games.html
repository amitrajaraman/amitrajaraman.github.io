--- layout: page title: (Some) games I like ---
  • Disco Elysium and 1000xRESIST: Is it weird for video games to be among my favorite books?
  • Celeste: Difficult but rewarding game that focuses on platforming.
  • Baba Is You and Bean and Nothingness: Probably the best puzzle games I've played.
  • Blue Prince and Tunic: Games that start simple, but have incredibly deep (puzzlehunt-style) puzzles.
  • Dark Souls and Sekiro: Difficult but rewarding games that focus on combat and exploration.
  • Titanfall 2 and ULTRAKILL: Two incredible shooters focusing on movement.
  • Hollow Knight and Silksong: Difficult but rewarding games that focus on exploration.
  • Dishonored and Prey: Really fun and immersive, you can mess around in them forever.
  • Shadow Tactics and Desperados III: Strategy games where the solutions make you feel smart.
